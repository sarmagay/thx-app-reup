What makes a good journaling experience?

Our target audience for this app is young adults who are returning to campus after living at home or abroad for the last six months. We wanted to focus on students who were experiencing adulthood for the first time during a global pandemic, and how journaling affected their mental health journey. While researching and interviewing our target audience, we came across three main issues.

Journal Options

We want to provide customizable journal entries that provide different options for color, size, and format in order to give visual representations of their days

Commitment

In order to encourage a consistent habit of self reflection through journaling, our platform will have self-set notifications that remind them to journal

Interactivity

Our platform will allow sharing entries like thank you notes and messages with others

Features

The Thx app is a journaling app. It creates a calm and uncluttered space for someone to start journaling or continue their journaling habits. We have several features that, through multiple user tests, facilitate good jounaling habits.

Personalized Notifications

The user is able to set alarms for when they want to journal including the time of day for each day of the week. This will allow them to remain consistent in their journaling journey.

Color of the Day

It's no secret that colors hold emotional and cultural significance. In order to create an environment that reflects the way they feel, users can pick a color that they think represents their general feelings for that day.

Charts

Bringing together the color of the day feature and the way journaling helps us remember the events of our days better, we created charts. A user can visually represent their daily mood for a certain date range in order to understand how they have been feeling over the course of time.

Music

From our research, we found that some people like to journal with music. Whether it's lowkey instrumentals or your favorite italian rock band, Thx app lets you connect the music you want to create the best journaling experience.

Thank-you notes

The user will be able to share their entries or write a specialised thank-you note to friends who may or may not use the app thanks to the share option that allows one to download the thank-you note as a picture.

Custom Journal Entries

We want to provide a variety of entry-options that the user can customize according to their liking.

Gallery

Our vision for this app was to create a simple and learnable interface that would help people want to journal and use journaling as a way to better their mental health. We wanted the app's color of the day feature to bring the pop of color while the general interface remained black and white.
